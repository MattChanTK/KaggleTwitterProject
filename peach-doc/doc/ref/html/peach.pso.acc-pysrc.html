 10  __doc__ = """ 
 11  Functions to update the velocity (ie, accelerate) of the particles in a swarm. 
 12   
 13  Acceleration of a particle is an important concept in the theory of particle 
 14  swarm optimizers. By choosing an adequate acceleration, particle velocity is 
 15  changed so that they can search the domain of definition of the objective 
 16  function such that there is a greater probability that a global minimum is 
 17  found. Since particle swarm optimizers are derived from genetic algorithms, it 
 18  can be said that this is what creates diversity in a swarm, such that the space 
 19  is more thoroughly searched. 
 20  """
