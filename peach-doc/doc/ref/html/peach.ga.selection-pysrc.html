 11  Basic classes and definitions for selection operator. 
 12   
 13  The first step in a genetic algorithm is the selection of the fittest 
 14  individuals. The selection method typically uses the fitness of the population 
 15  to compute which individuals are closer to the best solution. However, instead 
 16  of deterministically deciding which individuals continue to the next generation, 
 17  they are randomly choosen, the chances of an individual being choosen given by 
 18  its fitness value. This sub-module implements selection methods.

 41

 43      below: 
 44

 47          parameters can be used here to configure the operator. A default value 
 48          should always be offered, if possible. 
 49   
 50        __call__(self, population) 
 51          The ``__call__`` implementation should receive a population and operate 
 52          over it. Please, consult the ``ga`` module to see more information on
